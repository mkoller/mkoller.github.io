Introducing MeterHero Offsets

For many of us, water conservation is a tricky issue. We know that it’s important to use water efficiently, but it’s also not clear how much more efficient we should be! For example, we might agree that a 30-minute shower is too long. But what about 30 seconds? Is that too much to expect? Can we find a way to do the right thing without feeling guilty about not doing enough? Can we take a guilt-free shower?

Soon, MeterHero will introduce the first platform for offsetting your water use (we will eventually expand it to include electricity and natural gas as well). It’s a marketplace for buying and selling water offsets.

Here’s how it works: You have been doing your part by saving water, as documented by your water bill or from meter readings you have been taking. We’ll take your savings and put them on sale on the MeterHero marketplace.
