MeterHero

MeterHero.com

  • History
  • My Role

MeterHero makes it easy to track and share water and energy data, then pays you cash for conserving. MeterHero was a finalist in the Clean Energy Challenge, a charter class member of the Global Freshwater Seed Accelerator of the Water Council, and a winner of the Midwest Social Innovation prize.


Web Developer

I love having the opportunity to take on new and interesting projects. My recent work was co-founding a startup called MeterHero with a Political Science Professor at Marquette University. MeterHero is a Ruby on Rails application that calculates water conservation and rewards users via cash rebates when they save.
